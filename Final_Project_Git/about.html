What Can The Geography of Crime Tell Us?

Being large and diverse, the US has a range of different crime statistics by state. First let's look at the states with the highest total crimes committed.

Total Violent Crime By State

Total Property Crime By State

In both of these examples, California, Texas and New York stand out with high crime totals. However these states also have very large populations. It's more useful to examine the crime per capita as in the next example.

Total Violent Crime Per Capita By State

Total Property Crime Per Capita By State

Looking at crime rates per capita tells a very different story. Texas and California no longer stand out. However Alaska and New Mexico have very high crime per capita, for both property and violent crimes. We can also see that Louisiana, Tennessee and South Carolina have unusually high crime per capita.
